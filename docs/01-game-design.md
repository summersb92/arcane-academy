# 01 — Game Design Document

## 0. Structure: two acts
The game runs in two acts joined by a one-time transition. Act I is fully specified in
[08-origin-and-founding.md](08-origin-and-founding.md); this document covers the shared systems and Act II
(the academy) in depth.

- **Act I — The Long Road (solo).** A nobody labors for coin, discovers magic, studies cantrips, and makes
  a living as a hedge-mage taking Contracts and scribing scrolls. Same resources & verbs as Act II, smaller.
- **The Founding.** When Gold/Renown/Charter/Site thresholds are met, you found an academy. Your lair
  becomes the first room; your skills become the Headmaster's kit; everything carries over.
- **Act II — The Academy (management).** Rooms, students, faculty, the full research web, and Contracts at
  scale. This is where the long game and all prestige live.

> **There is no Blight / no wave-defense mechanic.** Tension comes from resource & morale balancing,
> capacity choices, and *opt-in* Contracts (positive pressure — rewards for engaging, never punishment for
> ignoring). This keeps the game cozy and idle-friendly.

## 1. Resources

Three groups, mirroring the UI frame (doc 04): **Resources** on the left, the **Character** on the right.
The full model — elements, vitals, the personal systems — is specified in
[09-magic-and-personal-systems.md](09-magic-and-personal-systems.md); this is the summary.

### Physical resources (LEFT panel · per-run, reset on Ascension)
| Resource | Symbol | Source | Sink |
|----------|--------|--------|------|
| **Gold** (coin) | ⦿ | Labor (Act I), tuition, scroll/potion sales, Contract rewards, patron graduates | Home fixtures, rooms, faculty salaries, crafting, the Founding, site upgrades |
| **Insight** | ◈ | Study, Home Study Desk, libraries, professors, studying students | The **Skills** web (formerly Research) |
| **Renown** | ★ | Contracts, Bestiary feats, graduating Futures, events; word-of-mouth in Act I | Attracts students/faculty; gates the Founding; soft win metric; feeds Ascension payout |
| **Materials** | (various) | Bestiary drops, gathering, contracts (Moonpetal, Iron, Spirit Dust, Wyrm Scale…) | Potions, Equipment crafting, inscribing spells |

### Vitals (RIGHT panel · the character)
| Vital | Symbol | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **Life** | ✚ | Health. Risked in Bestiary hunts & risky Contracts; being Downed is a setback, never game-over. |
| **Stamina** | ⚡ | Action/effort pool: labor (Act I), active abilities, exploring, hunts. Regenerates. |
| **Mana** | ✦ | The general pool you **cast spells** from. Regenerates. |

### Essence — the seven elements (RIGHT panel · the character)
Six base elements in three opposed pairs, plus **Prismatic** (the unified master element):
**▲ Fire ↔ ▼ Water · ⬢ Earth ↔ ≈ Air · ☀ Light ↔ ☾ Dark · ❖ Prismatic**.

Essence is generated by Home fixtures, Skills, equipment affinity, Bestiary drops, and (after the Founding)
Academy element-halls; it is spent to inscribe spells, brew tonics, and power typed effects. Favoring one
element mildly suppresses its opposite (specialize = strong-but-brittle); **balancing all six** unlocks
**Prismatic**, the endgame. See doc 09 §1.

> **Why elements instead of five schools?** This adopts **Arcanum's** elemental magic (and its combat/
> opposition), making the game a solo-mage RPG first; the Academy later *teaches* these same elements.
> In Act I you usually open only 1–2 elements.

### Meta (persist through Ascension — see doc 03)
| Resource | Symbol | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Aeon** | ∞ | Earned when you Ascend, ∝ accumulated Renown & graduates. Spent on the permanent meta-tree. |
| **Legacy** | ⌛ | Banked passively by *idle Archmages* on your roster (Arcanum's "Stored Time"). |
| **Attunement** | ◊ | Top-tier prestige currency from Ley Line collapse (deep endgame). |

## 2. The core loop (Act II)

```
        ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
        │                    IDLE ENGINE (ticks)                   │
        │   rooms + students → mana / gold / insight per second    │
        └───────────────┬─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                        │ you spend on…
     ┌──────────────────┼───────────────────┬─────────────────────┐
     ▼                  ▼                   ▼                     ▼
  BUILD/UPGRADE     RECRUIT             RESEARCH              CAST / RITUAL
  rooms (schools)   students+faculty    spell-theory web      headmaster spells
     │                  │                   │                     │
     └──────► students study, skills rise, then GRADUATE into ────┘
                        │
                 FUTURES (careers) → Renown + per-run boons + Contract power
                        │
              ┌─────────┴─────────┐
              ▼                   ▼
        CONTRACTS            MILESTONES
    (opt-in petitions:      (unlock next tier)
     dispatch students           │
     → Gold/Renown/mats)         │
              │                   │
              └────────► when growth plateaus ────► ASCEND (prestige)
                                                      → Aeon, meta-tree, new era
```

*(Act I is a compact version of this: your **person** is the generator, your **lair** the only room, and you
fulfill Contracts yourself. See doc 08.)*

**Short loop (seconds–minutes):** watch a rate, build/upgrade the room that unblocks the next thing.
**Medium loop (tens of minutes):** graduate a cohort; research a tier; fulfill a lucrative Contract.
**Long loop (first Founding, then 1–3h/run):** found the academy; later, Ascend and pick an Era Doctrine.
**Meta loop (days):** grow an Archmage roster; eventually Ley-Attune to raise the ceiling.

## 3. Systems overview

### 3.1 Rooms (the economy backbone)
- Every room belongs to one of the **five schools** (or is **Utility**/**Ambiance**).
- Rooms have **tiers/levels**; upgrading costs escalating gold + the school's mana.
- **Classrooms** generate mana *and* teach a skill to students inside them.
- **Utility rooms** (Library, Dormitory, Refectory, Vault, **Sanctum/Infirmary**) provide global effects
  (insight, student cap, morale, gold storage, mishap reduction).
- **Ambiance** (decorations, gardens, statues) provide a **morale/efficiency multiplier** — Spellcaster's
  decoration cards, abstracted.
- Rooms unlock via **Research** and **milestones**. A soft **spatial budget** (Grounds capacity, sized by
  your Site) forces choices; capacity is upgradeable.

### 3.2 Students (the idle multiplier)
- Students **auto-arrive** (rate scales with Renown + Dormitory capacity) and **auto-enroll** per your
  **Enrollment Policy** (e.g., "favor Shadow," "balanced," "toward Healer futures").
- Each student has a **race** and **traits** (Spellcaster) that modify learning speed and mana output —
  e.g., *Elf*: +learning; *Vampire*: ×2 Umbra; *Werewolf*: −disruption (fewer mishaps); *Deepfolk*: +Contract power.
- Students accumulate **skills** across schools while enrolled; skill thresholds make them eligible for **Futures**.
- **Graduation** removes the student and pays out **Renown**, a **per-run boon**, and possibly a **patron**
  (recurring gold) or a **veteran** (a strong recurring Contract-runner).

### 3.3 Faculty
- **Professors** boost a room/school (teaching rate, mana yield) and cost **gold upkeep**.
- Special staff (Mentor, Priest, Steward — from Spellcaster) grant global perks and can suppress problems.
- Hired from a rotating **candidate pool** (quality scales with Renown). Slots are limited; assignment is a decision.

### 3.4 Research — "Spell Theory" (Arcanum's depth)
- A branching **web/tree**: five colored trunks (one per school) plus a neutral trunk. It *grows out of* your
  Act I cantrip tree — the first few nodes are literally the cantrips you already learned.
- Nodes cost **Insight** (+ sometimes mana). They unlock rooms, spells, student capacity, multipliers,
  Futures, Contract types, and new mechanics.
- Deliberately **wide** and **inspectable** — the primary sink for Insight and the main in-run progression.

### 3.5 Spells & Rituals (the active layer)
- **Rituals**: toggleable ongoing effects that drain mana/stamina for a bonus (e.g., "Overclock the Ember
  Font": +50% ▲ Fire, −stamina/s).
- **Active spells**: instant Headmaster casts costing **Stamina** (+mana) — burst production, calm a mishap,
  speed a Contract, "inspire" a student to graduate now. Rewards check-ins without demanding them.
- **Scrolls** (Arcanum's scribing): craft one-shot consumables from essence + Materials to bank a boost.

### 3.6 Contracts (the outside world — replaces the old Blight)
- A rotating board of **Petitions/Contracts** from the world: heal a village, escort a caravan, cleanse a
  ruin, brew a royal tonic, tame a beast, recover a relic.
- Each has **requirements** (mana cost and/or a dispatched student/graduate with certain skills), a
  **duration** (idle timer), and **rewards** (Gold, Renown, rare crafting materials, occasional unlocks like
  a Charter or blueprint).
- **Act I:** you personally fulfill them (cost Stamina + mana + time) — your livelihood.
- **Act II:** **dispatch students/graduates** on them; combat/adventuring Futures (Battlemage, Assassin,
  Warden) excel at dangerous ones, giving those Futures a clear purpose. Away students don't produce mana
  while dispatched — a real trade-off.
- **Fully opt-in.** Ignoring Contracts costs you rewards, never HP. This is *positive* pressure, on purpose.
- **Light/Lumen's role:** powers restoration Contracts and reduces **Mishaps** (below); the Sanctum room
  and Priests lean on it.

### 3.7 Morale & Mishaps (light, cozy pressure)
- **Morale** is a global efficiency multiplier fed by Ambiance, the Refectory (food/upkeep), and Light.
- Low morale / overcrowding can trigger occasional **Mishaps** — small negative choice-events (a spat, a
  botched experiment) that nudge you to invest in amenities. Never catastrophic; always recoverable.
- This is the *entirety* of the game's negative pressure — deliberately gentle.

### 3.8 Events & Chronicle (flavor + variance)
- A running **Chronicle** narrates milestones, arrivals, graduations, discoveries, Contracts, and the Founding.
- **Choice events** (text vignettes, 2–3 options) inject variance and small risk/reward — a traveling
  merchant, a gifted orphan, a rival academy, a ley-line tremor.

## 4. Screens / tabs
Three-zone frame (doc 04): **Resources left · tabs top · Character right.** Top tabs:

1. **Main** — the **Activity / Tasks** screen (doc 10): the tasks your mage is running (in Activity slots)
   and the tasks available to start — labor, study, craft, hunt, contracts, rest, build — plus the Chronicle.
2. **Skills** — the progression web (formerly Research): Elementalism, Focus, Scribing, Alchemy, Combat, Scholarship.
3. **Player** — character sheet: level/XP, attributes (Might/Focus/Attunement), per-element resistances, titles.
4. **Home** — your dwelling & its fixtures (passive bonuses); holds the **Founding** card → becomes the academy's first hall.
5. **Spell Craft** — compose spells (element × shape × component) and inscribe them; your spellbook.
6. **Equipment** — gear slots (Focus/Robes/Ring/Amulet) + inventory; affinity & resistances.
7. **Potions** — brew consumables from Materials at the Alembic; held potions.
8. **Bestiary** — creatures to hunt/study/summon; the light combat & gathering layer (risks Life).
9. **Academy** — the institution (locked until the Founding): element-halls, Students, Faculty, Enrollment,
   Contracts-at-scale, and the **Ascension** meta-tree/Doctrine picker open from here.

*Act I* shows a reduced rail (Main · Skills · Player · Home, then Spell Craft/Equipment/Potions/Bestiary as
they unlock); **Academy** greys in at the Founding. **Codex** and **System** (save/export, settings,
offline report) are reachable from the header. Full wireframes in [04-ui-sketches.md](04-ui-sketches.md).

## 5. Explicit merge map (source element → our system)

### From Spellcaster University
| Spellcaster element | Becomes |
|---|---|
| 5 mana decks (Arcane/Nature/Light/Shadow/Alchemy) | Reframed onto **Arcanum's 6 elements + Prismatic** (doc 09); academy **halls teach elements** |
| Classroom / Utility / Decoration cards | Room categories: element-Hall, Utility, Ambiance (tiers instead of RNG cards) |
| Card **draw** RNG | Optional flavor: "Inspiration Draws" — occasional free blueprint/relic picks (a nod, not the core) |
| Students auto-pick classes | Enrollment Policy + auto-assign |
| Races & traits (elf, vampire, werewolf, deepfolk…) | Student traits = idle modifiers on learning, element affinity & Contracts |
| Graduation **Futures** (hero, assassin, lich…) | Futures granting Renown + per-run boons + Contract power |
| Faculty (mentor, priest, steward) | Faculty hires with global perks |
| Faction reputation (Orcs, Inquisition, guilds) | Renown + faction standings surfaced via choice events & Contracts |
| Sending graduates into the world | **Contracts / expeditions** (positive-pressure objectives) |
| Rooms need a mana-type to unlock | School-gated research + build costs |

### From Arcanum: Theory of Magic
| Arcanum element | Becomes |
|---|---|
| **Rise from stable-hand → Archmage** | **Act I: the Origin Arc** (doc 08) — the literal opening of the game |
| Learn spells/skills; elements with different intricacies | **Skills** web + **Spell Craft** (element×shape×component) + 6 elements + Prismatic (doc 09) |
| Life / Stamina / Mana; elemental essences | The **Character** panel: ✚ Life ⚡ Stamina ✦ Mana + 7 essences (doc 09) |
| Scribe scrolls; craft equipment; brew potions | **Spell Craft** (inscribe) + **Equipment** + **Potions** tabs |
| Dungeons / combat / opposition | **Bestiary** hunts (risk Life; bring the opposite element) + combat-oriented student Futures |
| Deep tooltips, tables, text UI | The entire aesthetic |
| **Wizard's Hall**: multiple characters, no hard reset, idle "Stored Time" | **Archmage Roster** + **Legacy** banking (doc 03); each Archmage has their own Act I origin |
| Prestige that doesn't wipe everything | Ascension keeps meta-tree/Codex; characters persist |

## 6. First-session flow (target onboarding)
1. **Origin.** You're a stable-hand with a few coins and some Vigor. Labor trickles Gold. (~2–4 min)
2. **The spark.** A scripted beat hands you a torn grimoire page → **Study** unlocks; Insight begins.
3. **Awakening.** Learn *Spark* (first ▲ Fire essence trickle) and a 2nd cantrip. Furnish your **Home**.
4. **Hedge-mage.** Scribe your first scroll for coin; take your first **Contract** (ward a barn) → +Gold, +Renown.
5. **Word spreads.** Renown climbs; a "Founding" card appears showing the four requirements.
6. **The Founding** (~15–40 min in): buy a Site + Charter → the academy is born; students begin arriving,
   the full tab rail unlocks, rooms/faculty/research open. *This is the "aha, now it's a whole game" moment.*
7. **~1–3h:** academy grows; first Futures graduate; growth plateaus; **Ascension** unlocks with a real Aeon
   payout and a Doctrine choice → run 2 (which can fast-forward Act I via the meta-tree).
