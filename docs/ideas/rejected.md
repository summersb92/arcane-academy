# Rejected Ideas (0/3 — don't add)

From the [roundtable](roundtable.md). Zero KEEP votes — all three designers cut these (often including the
originator). Recorded so they don't get re-proposed; the reason is the useful part.

- **M3 · Byproduct Pipelines** *(Vex's own — cut by all, incl. Vex)* — tasks emit a secondary material only
  another task consumes; unconsumed byproduct decays.
  *Rejected: a decay clock + supply-chain balancing turns a cozy text-idler into a factory sim and adds the
  exact punishment timer the game swore off. Vex killed his own darling.*

- **M11 · The Two Hands** *(Rook's own — cut by all)* — a left/right-hand stance toggle; opposite-element
  pairs across hands earn "Tension," balanced pairs earn "Flow."
  *Rejected: a stance/positioning meta that fights "click a card and walk away." The accepted **M2 Adjacency
  Synergies** delivers the opposition-on-the-board goal without the choreography.*

- **M12 · Conjunctions** *(Rook's own — cut by all)* — a celestial-alignment strip periodically buffs one
  tag and dampens its opposite; time your task starts to it.
  *Rejected: a timed FOMO window that punishes the away-from-keyboard player — the antithesis of idle-first.
  Rook conceded it was "a timed punish wearing a cozy robe."*

- **M13 · Rituals / card-combos** *(Rook's own — cut by all)* — drag 2–3 specific cards onto a "Circle" to
  fuse them into a temporary super-task.
  *Rejected: a drag-to-fuse minigame bolted onto the task grid; fights the static/text frame and bloats
  scope. Rook self-cut.*

- **C1 · Standing Attunement** *(Vex's own — cut by all, incl. Vex)* — every inscribed spell trickle-drains
  its element's essence to stay castable, else it greys out.
  *Rejected: makes your spellbook a maintenance chore that taxes idle income and punishes collecting spells.
  The accepted **C11 Grimoire Loadout** achieves "spellbook width has a cost" via page slots instead.*

- **C14 · Misinscription = Discovery** *(Rook's own — cut by all)* — inscribing an unstable combo may fail
  "interestingly" into a weaker but unique "wild" spell.
  *Rejected: RNG unique-spell generation is a content/balance rabbit hole, and it rewards spamming inscribes.
  The accepted **C13 Word Roots** gives intentional, player-driven variety instead. Rook self-cut.*

# Round 2 (Fable) — 0/3 rejected

- **R2-C11 · The House Remembers** *(Rook's own — cut by all, incl. Rook)* — casting milestones burn a
  "Sigil" fixture into your Home bearing the spell's coined name, trickling its element.
  *Rejected: the shallower cousin of the accepted **R2-C2 Hearth-Binding** fighting over the same Home

  fixture labels.*

# Round 3 (Longevity Council) — 0/3 rejected

- **K15 · First Spark Variants** *(Tempo's own — cut by all, incl. Tempo)* — a 1-of-3 inciting-spark draft

  *Rejected as a separate system: it's a thinner **K6 Lineages**, and two back-to-back 1-of-3 drafts at run
